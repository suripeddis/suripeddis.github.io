About me

CS student who cares deeply about impact.

I'm a 21-year-old student at Northeastern University majoring in computer science with a focus on artificial intelligence. It's not simply technology that motivates me. It is the meeting point of true human good and innovation. Every line of code I write should improve someone's life, not merely function well on paper.

The messy, human issues that technology frequently ignores particularly appeal to me: the times when people feel trapped, overburdened, or ignored. I'm most interested in work that meets people where they are, whether that means assisting non-speaking students in finding their voice or creating tools that transform complicated paperwork into clarity and peace of mind.

At the moment, this entails delving deeply into machine learning, retrieval-augmented generation, and full-stack programming, learning how to take an idea from a basic notion to something that actual people can use. I'm constantly searching for the next task worth tackling because I enjoy the challenge of creating things that are both technically challenging and truly compassionate.
